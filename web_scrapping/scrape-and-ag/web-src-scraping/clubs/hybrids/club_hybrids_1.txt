WHY HYBRID GOLF CLUBS ARE A MUST-HAVE
Hybrid clubs are the perfect blend of long irons and woods. Designed to have an easier launch with greater
forgiveness, these new hybrid golf clubs are great for golfers of all levels. Hybrids combine the best features of an
iron with the fairway wood giving golfers the added flexibility to handle the course.
KEY FEATURES OF HYBRID GOLF CLUBS
+ Center of Gravity: The clubhead design helps keep a lower center of gravity helping the ball get airborne
quickly and more accurately. The easier launch is perfect for golfers looking for a more accurate shot while
maneuvering tougher situations on the course
+ Usage: The usage can range anywhere from the tee, rough, fairway and even out of bunkers. With hybrid
golf clubs, golfers can hit themselves out of any situation on the course which makes it a must-have for any
bag
« Adjustability:Adjustability and hybrids go hand-in-hand. Adjust the club to meet the demand of any golfer,
giving them a distinct advantage on the course. Making adjustments to the loft and lie angles can only
benefits golfers at all levels
